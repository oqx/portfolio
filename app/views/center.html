Center

After many design and data iterations, a minimum viable product was born. Center is a one-stop integration hub, allowing users to connect their marketing stack, track leads as they travel through the pipeline, and store lead data.


Myself and a small team were elected to make Center a tangible product, giving us the opportunity to implement methodologies and structure to create a maintainable code base. On top of using BEM and ES6, we created an internal CSS/Sass framework to improve PR velocity and code quality. No need to recreate the wheel, right?

The framework of Center was Aurelia + Gulp to begin with, but we eventually moved toward Webpack for bundling. If you haven't used Aurelia, I highly recommend it.
